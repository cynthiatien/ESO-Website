What is Elementary Science Olympiad?


Elementary Science Olympiad (ESO) is a science competition consisting of 13 events that cover a variety of science topics. Students of any potential can compete in these events. Teams comprised of 10-15 students can compete as partners or groups of 3 in 13 separate events that consists of build, lab, and study events.

Family fun day of science, competition, activities, guest speakers, and more.




Frequently Asked Questions


Who can join?

As this is our first year hosting ESO, we are opening the competition to all fourth and fifth graders in the Pleasanton Unified School District.

Where and when will the competition be?

At Amador Valley High School, from 10 AM - 3 PM

How much will it cost?

We are asking for a $10 donation for each student

How much time commitment is it for each student?

Students should complete everything necessary through one hour meetings.

Will we need additional meetings outside of the current one-hour ones?

We are aiming it so that students don't have to spend additional time.
